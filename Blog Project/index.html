Welcome to our wilfully lonely world...

The Anti-social Butterfly is a safe place for all of the girlies that are people persons but prefer solitude. It's a haven for anti-social extroverts, like myself, who prefer solo dates over group dates. For the girls who are naturally people magnets, but find it emotionally draining. This one is for those of us that love to travel, are foodies and suckers for authentic experiences but prefer to take it on in more intimate ways. There is space in this world for you, and its right here. Sit back, recharge and explore with us on our solo adventures!! Enjoy!!
