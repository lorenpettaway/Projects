About the creator...

Loren M Pettaway

Owner/Operator of Butterfly Sanctuary, LLC

Hello!! My name is Loren, and I am the founder and owner of The Anti-Social Butterfly! I was raised in New Orleans, Louisiana as the baby and only girl of the family. Being the youngest amongst all boys, I was propelled into the wonderful world of solitude. I found enjoyment in living a world powered only by my imagination. As a little girl, I found myself builing an imaginary world where I could explore whenever and however I wanted...and as an adult, ive found ways to make those daydreams my daily reallity. Throughout life, ive found some pretty amazing and like-minded companions. So, please join us on our indivual and mutual adventures!

It is not who you are underneath, but what you do that defines you.
